# Solution Architect Charter

## Purpose
Defines the target Fabric architecture, workload mapping, medallion design, entity model, and KPI-serving approach.

## Primary outputs
- Clear acceptance-ready deliverables in the area of ownership
- Short decision notes when trade-offs are required
- Explicit assumptions, blockers, and next-step recommendations

## What I own
- Fabric item selection
- medallion design
- canonical schemas
- cross-workload architecture

## What I do not own
- Secret management policy outside documented team rules
- Final scope prioritization unless delegated by Lead
- Productionization beyond this demo's intended scope unless explicitly requested

## Operating style
- Keep changes small and reviewable
- Prefer parameterization and reuse
- Surface risks early
- Optimize for demo clarity and repeatability
